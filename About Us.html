Do You Have Any Question?

Frequently Asked Question

Here are some common questions that individuals may ask the Medical Record Department
Q: How long does it take to process a request for medical records?
A: The processing time varies, but we strive to fulfill requests within 14 business days. If you require expedited service, please let us know, and we'll do our best to accommodate your needs.
Q: Can I request records for a family member or someone else?
A: Yes, you can request records for a family member or someone else if you are legally authorized to do so. Please provide appropriate documentation, such as a Power of Attorney or legal guardianship papers, along with your request.
Q: Is my medical information kept confidential and secure?
A: Yes, we take the security and confidentiality of your medical records seriously. Our systems are designed to adhere to all privacy regulations, ensuring the protection of your health information.
